# Skill: BRD Template Reference

> Inject into Agent 02 (BRD Writer). Provides the canonical 14-section BRD structure.

## BRD section checklist

- [ ] 1. Executive Summary
- [ ] 2. Business Context & Objectives (2.1 Background, 2.2 Current State, 2.3 Future State, 2.4 Objectives, 2.5 Benefits)
- [ ] 3. Scope (In / Out / Assumptions)
- [ ] 4. Stakeholders & Roles
- [ ] 5. Business Rules (BR-001... with ID, rule, rationale, regulatory source, owner)
- [ ] 6. Process Flows (narrative — no PEGA jargon)
- [ ] 7. Regulatory & Compliance Requirements (mapped to process)
- [ ] 8. Data Requirements (entities, quality rules, retention)
- [ ] 9. Integration Requirements (business level — no technical detail)
- [ ] 10. Non-Functional Requirements
- [ ] 11. Assumptions, Dependencies & Constraints
- [ ] 12. Risks & Issues
- [ ] 13. Glossary
- [ ] 14. Document Control & Sign-off

## Business rule numbering

BR-001 sequential. Group by area:
- BR-001–019: Customer identification
- BR-020–039: Risk scoring
- BR-040–059: Approval and routing
- BR-060–079: Integrations
- BR-080–099: Reporting and audit
