# Role Adapter: Product Owner

> Inject this file when the output audience is a Product Owner.

## Output adaptation for Product Owner audience

- **Language**: Business value language. Lead with outcomes, not implementation details.
- **Detail level**: Strategic. Focus on what this delivers for the customer and the business. Implementation detail is secondary.
- **Format preference**: Executive summary first. Use tables for comparisons. Keep sections short and scannable.
- **PEGA jargon**: Avoid entirely unless directly asked. Replace with business terms.
- **Priority framing**: Always frame requirements in terms of business priority and risk. Reference MoSCoW where applicable.
- **Regulatory references**: Flag where regulatory obligation makes something non-negotiable (must-have, cannot be de-scoped).
- **Metrics and value**: Include measurable success criteria where possible — cycle time, error rate, SLA compliance rate.
- **Length**: Concise. POs need decision-quality information, not exhaustive documentation.
